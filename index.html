Astrocat

How to play

You are the wonderful Astrocat Tristan! Your home planet, Yarntopia, has been invaded by the evil Woofians. In order to save Yarntopia you must collect the fish in order to feed the Meowians. They are weak from hunger and need their strength in order to fight off the Woofian army. Yarntopia is slowly unraveling due to this Catostrophic war! The core of the planet is very toxic and will kill you on impact. You must collect all of the fish in order to move onto the next level. Do not touch the yellow lava or the Woofians! Fortunatley due to your genetics you have 9 lives! Use them wisely. Save the planet. It is in your paws meow.
